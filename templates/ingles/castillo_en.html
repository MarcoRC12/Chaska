Lamas Castle

“Delve into a magical corner in the heart of the Amazon: the Lamas Castle. This imposing medieval-style structure, unique in Peru, stands majestically on the hills of Lamas, offering an experience that blends history, art and fantasy. From its towers you can observe impressive landscapes, and inside you will find murals, sculptures and details that narrate ancestral legends and the vision of a dream come true. It is a place where the European and the Amazonian embrace each other, ideal for taking unforgettable photographs and living an experience that seems to be taken from a fairy tale.”
