Wayku Community

"Live the living soul of Lamas in the indigenous community of El Wayku, home of the Quechua-Lamista people. Here you will not only learn about their traditions, but you will be part of them. Walking through its streets, you will hear the ancestral language, see their colorful traditional costumes and discover rituals, dances, food and stories passed down from generation to generation. If you are looking for authentic and transformative tourism, El Wayku is an experience that touches the heart."
